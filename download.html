Why it matters

Reducing food waste in schools

Plate2Purpose empowers educational institutions to tackle food waste through innovative tracking and management solutions. The app helps schools monitor consumption patterns, reduce waste, and educate students about sustainable practices, supporting UN Sustainable Development Goal 12 while creating a positive impact in your school community.
